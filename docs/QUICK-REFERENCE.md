## Sample Booking Letter

```
Dear [Venue Manager],

I am writing to request permission to play music at your venue on [date and time]. I am a musician and would like to perform a collection of folk-rock and soft rock with a sprinkling of pop, country-rock, and original material for your guests.

A demo of my music can be found at glennbennett.com/samples, where you can learn more about me and my work. I have also included a list of equipment I will bring to the venue for the performance.

I believe that my music would be an excellent fit for your audience, and I would love to have the opportunity to perform for them. I am willing to work with you to ensure that the performance is a success and that all rules and regulations are followed.

I provide my own PA system and can bring a power supply if needed.

Please let me know if you require any additional information or if you have any questions. I look forward to hearing back from you soon.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
Glenn Bennett
```
